Appointments

If you are in need of free clothing for your school-aged Snohomish County child or children, we are here to help. Please use this appointment tool to let us know about your family's needs and select an appointment window to pick up clothing.

You will need to make one appointment and fill out one selection form for each child you are requesting clothing for.

Before you begin, please have available:

  • Your email address
  • First name and age of child
  • Preferred gender for clothing
  • Clothing sizes

You will also be given an opportunity to give us a brief qualitative description of your child's preferences. For older children/teens, we recommend you fill out the form with the child present. Our goal is to provide your child with a small wardrobe of items that they will feel good about wearing. We cannot promise to fulfill specific requests, but we do try to personalize our clothing selections the best we can.

Volunteering

How It Works

We offer free clothing on a seasonal basis to school-aged children residing in Snohomish County. Size availability varies depending upon donations, but in general we stock sizes from 3T up to adult XXL (for teens).

Each child may receive a set of gently used clothing up to three times per year. At this time, the set consists of:

  • 3 shirts
  • 2 sweatshirts
  • 1 coat
  • 1-2 pairs of sweatpants (boys) or leggings (girls)
  • 3 pairs of underwear (new)
  • 3 pairs of socks (new)

As a temporary health and safety measure during the current pandemic, clothing selections will be made by our volunteers using parent guidance provided on the appointment form. Parents will pick up pre-chosen clothing from our location by appointment only.

To receive clothing for your child, please make an appointment using our online tool. As a part of the appointment process, you will be be asked questions about your child's age and clothing sizes and preferences. Get more information or begin your appointment process here.
