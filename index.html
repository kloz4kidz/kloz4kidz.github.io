Appointments

If you are in need of free clothing for your school-aged Snohomish County child or children, we are here to help. We are now scheduling contactless clothing pick-ups for our clients.

Directions for the appointment process are below. Before you begin, please have your child's clothing sizes available.

  1. Click the 'Book Now' button below and select an appointment time. Please choose a time you will be available to come to Marysville to pick up your child's clothing.
  2. Each child will receive 3 shirts, 2 sweatshirts, 1 coat, 2 pairs of sweatpants, leggings (girls only), 3 pairs of sock and 3 pairs of underwear. At this time, jeans and shoes will not be available.
  3. Fill out the appointment form. Be sure to provide us with information about each child you are making a request for. Click Continue at the bottom of the form to make your appointment request.
  4. You should receive an email right away indicating that you have submitted your request. Within a day or two you should receive another email confirming your appointment.
  5. On your appointment day, come to the Kloz 4 Kidz portable located behind Marysville United Methodist Church. Please plan to arrive on time for your appointment. Please do not come early or arrive late. Appointment times are spaced for health, safety, and privacy or our clients. If you are not able to make your appointment you can call us at 360-658-1021 or use the link provided in your confirmation email to cancel or reschedule.
  6. Give your name to the volunteer at the window beside the front porch and she will bring your bag(s) out onto the porch. Once she has gone back inside, you may collect your clothing bag(s). Please do not go up onto the porch while our volunteers are still outside. This is for your safety as well as theirs. Once you have your bags, please leave promptly, as others will be arriving for appointments or waiting for service.

How It Works

We offer free clothing on a seasonal basis to school-aged children residing in Snohomish County. Size availability varies depending upon donations, but in general we stock sizes from 4T up to teen/adult XXL.

A child may receive clothing from us every 4 months (maximum 3 times per year). At each visit, the child will receive a set of gently used, school-appropriate clothing, consisting of:

  • 3 shirts
  • 2 sweatshirts
  • 1 coat
  • 2-3 pairs of sweatpants and/or leggings (girls only)
  • 3 pairs of underwear (new)
  • 3 pairs of socks (new)

As a temporary health and safety measure during the current pandemic, clothing selections will be made by our volunteers using parent guidance provided on the appointment form. Parents will pick up pre-chosen clothing from our location by appointment only.

At this time, since we are unable to allow client to try on items, we are temporarily unable to provide shoes or jeans. These items will return to availability when we are able to safely open our building to shoppers.

To receive clothing for your child, please make an appointment using our online tool. As a part of the appointment process, you will be be asked questions about your child's age and clothing sizes and preferences. Get more information or begin your appointment process here.
